How We Measure Accuracy

What is a Signal?

When breaking news hits, our AI instantly analyzes it and generates a Bullish (price will go up) or Bearish (price will go down) signal for the affected asset.

How is Accuracy Measured?

We record the live market price at the exact second the news breaks. After the AI's dynamically predicted evaluation window has passed (or after the next market open for overnight/weekend equity news), we fetch the price again to see if the asset moved in the direction we predicted.

Correct Signals
-
Predicted direction matched the price action at the end of the evaluation window.
Incorrect Signals
-
Market moved opposite to our AI prediction.
Neutral signals (price didn't change) are excluded from the accuracy percentage.
